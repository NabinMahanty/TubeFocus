Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the TubeFocus browser extension handles your personal information. TubeFocus is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected and that your browsing experience remains fully secure.

1. Data Collection and Transmission

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data, browsing history, or settings to any external servers or third parties.

All operations performed by the TubeFocus extension happen locally on your computer. Specifically:

  • No Remote Servers: There are no remote databases or analytical services linked to the extension. No statistics, inputs, or channel logs ever leave your machine.
  • Local Settings Storage: Your preferences, such as enabled categories, custom keywords, whitelisted channels, focus statistics, and Pomodoro configurations, are saved locally in your browser profile.
  • Syncing Option: If you are logged into your Google Chrome browser profile and have sync enabled, your settings will be synced across your devices via Google's secure chrome.storage.sync API. This data is handled entirely by Google's infrastructure and is not accessible to the developers of TubeFocus.

2. Permissions Used and Rationale

To deliver distraction-free filtering and focus tools, TubeFocus requests the following browser permissions. Here is exactly why they are needed:

  • storage: Required to save your configuration preferences, category keyword toggles, focus timer settings, and streak metrics in your browser.
  • activeTab: Temporarily evaluates video element text metadata on the currently loaded YouTube tab when filtering is active.
  • alarms: Used to run the Pomodoro focus timer in the background so that sessions don't interrupt or reset if you close the extension popup.
  • Host Permissions (https://www.youtube.com/* & https://youtube.com/*): Grants permission to scan and hide distracting video containers on YouTube domains.

3. Third-Party Websites and Services

TubeFocus interacts with YouTube elements on your screen but is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or partner to YouTube or Google LLC. We do not control or access how YouTube tracks your interaction. We recommend checking YouTube's official Privacy Policy for information regarding how they handle your data.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to align with new features or store policy adjustments. Any changes will be reflected in updates to this document and uploaded alongside new extension releases.
